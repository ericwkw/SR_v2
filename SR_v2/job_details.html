Front-end Developer

We are in search of a rock star Front-end Developer – someone who is passionate about bringing great designs to life on the web. At Sedgwick Richardson there is no shortage of brilliant design minds. We now need someone with a solid technical grounding and a keen interst in the design and creative space to meet the growing need for online development.

Key responsibilities

•

•


•

Reporting to the Digital Director, your primary focus is on front-end development and light CMS development using open source PHP solutions such as WordPress

Be responsible to develop high performing front-end code using proven frameworks such as Bootstrap and Foundation — ensuring that it all works beautifully across various devices (mobile, desktop and tablet) and browsers

Work closely together with designers, projecte managers and the client servicing team to determin suitable technical solutions for our client’s requirements

Requirements

•

•

•

•

•

•

•

Degree in Computer Science or related discipline (preferably with a creative component) with at least 1–2 years relevant experience

Self-starter who doesn’t require a lot of supervision. You like to experiement with new technologies and show it off to the team

Excellent communication skills. You know how to translate development jargon into plain English or non-techies

Proficiency with HTML, CSS and Javascript and responsive front-end frameworks (i.i. Bootstrap)

Solid understanding of search engine optimisation and the ability to embed best practices into the website you build.

This includes explaining to non-technical people how SEO works and why it is beneficial

Experience using Grunt or other tast runners to automate processes — as well as experience in optimising sites for performance
